About

Hi! I am Yuyang He, an undergraduate student at the School of Materials Science and Engineering, Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST). I have a deep passion for physics and aspire to pursue a Ph.D. in the field. My research interests lie in condensed matter physics — particularly quantum computing, quantum information, and topological structures.

I approach life with energy and curiosity. I enjoy tracing ideas back to first principles and exploring the history of science — there is a quiet joy in understanding where ideas come from. I look forward to challenging myself, learning continuously, and thinking independently. Feel free to reach me at {{ site.author.email }}.
